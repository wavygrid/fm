FAQ

Most asked questions.

We have the most answers you're looking for, in more ways than one. Ask questions, browse around for answers, or submit your feature requests.

How can I join?

We are an exclusive invite only platform where you can join once invited by Facilitypal or a client in our network.

If you’ve received an invitation, you are part of a select group of service providers that your client wants to continue working with.

I haven't been invited, but I like to a join Facilitypal.

Simply contact us to see if you qualify for membership.

What are the requirements to join?

1. You must represent a registered company with a proven facility work history.
2. Must have at least 5 full time employees who are ready to be deployed.
3. Must have a registered corporate bank account with a Sri Lankan bank.
4. Each deployment staff must produce Police Clearance Certificate, Medical Report and must be Covid-19 negative.

How much does it cost?

There are no monthly fees, service charges and processing fees. We charge you a fixed commission fee of 15% on every completed work order we give you.

How do I get paid?

Once you complete your maintenance task, your earnings will be transferred automatically to your account, which can be withdrawn within 14 to 25 days. You can get paid instantly for work orders under Rs. 5,000.00. (Same day direct bank transfers.)

I am an independent contractor. Can I join?

If you are an expert with over 5 years of service or a recent graduate from a professional training institution, you can apply to become an expert independent contractor.
